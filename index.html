Hello! We're Pretty Wasted. A nu-metal and metalcore band from Ipswich England

About Us

We are a 4-piece nu-metal and metalcore band based in Ipswich, England, blending raw aggression with groove laced heaviness. Known for our explosive live energy, we've sold out the smokehouse not once, but twice and left the walls shaking every time.

Our setlist features not just originals, but reworked covers torn down and rebuilt in our own chaotic, genre blurring style. If you can't catch us on stage, we're in the rehearsal room writing and refining new material, working toward our debut releases.

Whether you're a pit veteran or just here for the riffs, welcome to the noise we're just getting started.
